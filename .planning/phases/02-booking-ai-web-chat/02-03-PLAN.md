      3. Parse body as `{ shopId: string; sessionId: string; message: string; consentGranted: boolean }`.

      9. Write `data: {"type":"done"}\n\n` then `reply.raw.end()`.
      10. After each request, call `cleanupExpiredSessions()` (lazy cleanup, no background timer).
